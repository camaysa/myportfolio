Computer Technology Honors & Awards Awards and Certifications

Industry and State Level Certificates

Throughout my high school career, I have earned many certifications that relate to computer technology. For each computer class I have taken in high school I have earned the CTE State Skills Certificate. This certifications demonstrate that I have learned the skills and knowledge at the required proficiency level mandatated by the state.

In addition to taking the state tests for the courses I have taken, I have also had the opportunity to take some industry level certifications. I have successfully passed the Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop exams in the last two years. I also recently passed the Microsoft Technoloy Associate exam for the Introduction to Programming using HTML and CSS. All of these certifications are a testament to my knowledge and skills earned in the courses I have taken.

Awards

As part of our local FBLA chapter I have had the opportunity to serve as a chapter officer as well as compete in various events. This year I chose the event, Publication Design. For this event I was required to create promotion material for a fresh food home delivery business as well as come up with a business name. I chose to create a logo, advertisement poster, business card, holiday flyers, and a meal recipe card using various Adobe programs.

This event could be comprised of an individual or up to a team of three people. I chose to do it by myself, even though I knew how much work it would be and knowing that I would have to present it by myself as well. The result was that I placed 2nd at our region competition and 7th at our state competition, but more importantly I have walked away from this experience with the knowledge, confidence and skills to be more successful in future projects and experiences like these.
