Computer Techonology and Camaysa Crittenden

Hello, my name is Camaysa Crittenden. I was born August 3, 2000 in Grand Junction, Colorado.

I am currently a senior in high school and I have been in love with Computer technology all of my life. My mom has been a computer teacher all my life and I have always loved to watch her and was very fascinated by the things that she was capable of doing on the computer.

This interest sparked me to want to embark on a journey to find out as much as I could when it came to computers. So as soon as I hit the 5th grade, when I was finally able to take a computer class, I joined my moms class. For the next 8 years, I enjoyed and learned more and more. I have taken so many computer classes that I have developed such great skills, people are often amazed at the abilities I have as such a young age.

I like to use computers and I use them for just about everything. My favorite thing to do is to use programs such as Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop to create and play with images.
