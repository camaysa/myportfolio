Leadership & Service Community Service and Leadership Activities

Community Garden Clean-up

My favorite community service activity was during the summer after my junior year. I attended the ACT bootcamp that was held in Price, UT. This was a week long event that was organized to help students in preparing for the ACT.

While there we had a great opportunity to help the community in Price, UT by working at a local community garden. The local garden was ran by elderly people who grew vegetables and used those to give to people who couldn’t afford food. As a group we went in and helped them to weed, plant, plow, water and more.

CTE Dance/Scholarship Fundraiser

As part of FBLA, I collaborated with the other school’s Career and Technical Student Organizations (CTSOs) to organize a CTE dance for CTE month. Chapter officers from FBLA, SkillsUSA, and FFA worked for about two months to plan and organize a dance for CTE month.

Utah Food Bank

I volunteer at the local food bank one Wednesday a month. Duties include separating and distributing food to various community members.
